Our Story

Founded in 2020, Ludo Game was created by a team of passionate game developers who wanted to bring the classic board game into the digital age while maintaining its charm and social elements.

What started as a small project quickly grew into one of the most popular mobile games, with millions of players worldwide enjoying our unique take on this timeless classic.

Our mission is to bring people together through fun, easy-to-play games that can be enjoyed by everyone, regardless of age or gaming experience.
