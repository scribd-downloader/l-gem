Last Updated: July 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ludo Game ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and password.
  • Profile information: You may provide additional information such as a profile picture and biography.
  • Communication: When you contact us or participate in surveys, we collect your name, email address, and any information you provide in your communications.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: We collect information about your device, including device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage data: We collect information about how you interact with our Services, including game statistics, preferences, and settings.
  • Location information: With your permission, we may collect and process information about your approximate location.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Communicate with you about products, services, offers, promotions, and events
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Personalize and improve your experience

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information as follows:

  • With service providers: We share information with third-party vendors and service providers who work on our behalf.
  • For legal reasons: We may share information if we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.
  • With your consent: We may share information with third parties when you give us consent to do so.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as a business asset.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to our Services
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you interact with our Services
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, some parts of our Services may not function properly if you disable cookies.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

International Data Transfers

We may transfer your information to countries other than the one in which you live. We rely on mechanisms such as standard contractual clauses to lawfully transfer data across borders.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal information
  • Objecting to our use of your personal information
  • Restricting certain processing of your personal information
  • Portability of your personal information
  • Withdrawing consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
