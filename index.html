How to Play

Master the game with these simple steps

Step 1
01

Roll the Dice

Start your turn by rolling the dice. You need to roll a 6 to bring a token out from your home. If you roll a 6, you get an extra turn! Use this strategically to advance your tokens quickly or bring out multiple tokens.

Pro Tip:

Always try to get all four tokens out as soon as possible for more movement options.

Step 2
02

Move Your Tokens

After rolling, move one of your tokens the corresponding number of spaces in a clockwise direction. If you land on a space occupied by an opponent's token, their token is sent back to their home!

Pro Tip:

Try to keep your tokens at safe spots (colored stars) to prevent them from being sent back home.

Step 3
03

Enter the Home Stretch

Once your token has traveled all the way around the board, it enters your colored home stretch. You must roll the exact number needed to reach the center. If you roll too high, you cannot move that token.

Pro Tip:

Position multiple tokens near your home stretch to increase your chances of getting tokens home.

Step 4
04

Win the Game

The first player to get all four tokens to the center wins the game! Victory requires both strategy and a bit of luck. Will you be able to outplay your opponents?

Pro Tip:

Balance between offense (sending opponents home) and defense (advancing your tokens) for the best chance of winning.
